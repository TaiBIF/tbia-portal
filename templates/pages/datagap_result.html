{% if LANGUAGE_CODE == 'en-us' %} In 2025, the Taiwan Biodiversity Information Alliance (TBIA) launched a thematic project dedicated to filling data gaps. Working hand in hand with its partner organizations, the alliance targeted different types of deficiencies, including taxonomic gaps, spatial gaps, and temporal gaps. This year, a total of 852 grid cells were filled, including 36 newly completed grid cells! The overall data coverage across Taiwan has now reached 78.97%. The dataset includes 3,838,564 records of protected species, with the top five taxonomic groups being amphibians, birds, ferns, mammals, and fish.
Below is the interactive section showcasing the results of the 2025 data gap-filling initiative. To ensure the integrity of the results, final statistics for all survey records generated in 2025 were compiled and finalized by the end of February 2026. {% else %} TBIA於2025年啟動資料空缺填補主題計畫，與聯盟各夥伴單位攜手合作，針對不同類型的空缺進行補充，包含物種空缺(Taxonomic Gaps)、空間空缺(Spatial Gaps)、時間空缺(Temporal Gaps)。本年度共填補了852個網格，其中成功新填補了36個網格！資料填補全台覆蓋率達78.97%。其中包含3,838,564筆保育類資料，前五大類群為兩棲類、鳥類、蕨類植物、哺乳類、魚類。
以下為2025年度資料空缺填補成果互動區，為確保空缺填補成果之完整性，所有於2025年產出之調查紀錄，統一於2026年2月底完成最終統計。 {% endif %}

{% if LANGUAGE_CODE == 'en-us' %} Temporal gaps indicate a lack of data during specific periods. These data discontinuities along the timeline can be filled by digitizing archival collections or by promoting the open access of historical and research surveys. The side-by-side bar charts compare the data counts across different years and months, demonstrating the continuity and completeness of the data over time, while also illustrating the current status of temporal data gaps. {% else %} 時間空缺表示某些時期內的資料不足，透過典藏資料數位化或加強開放過往調查或研究調查，皆能填補時間軸上的資料斷層。左右相互對照的長條圖呈現不同年份與月份的資料筆數，可顯示資料在時間軸上的連續性與完整性，並用以了解時間上的資料空缺狀態。 {% endif %}
